    How to change a tire
  1. Locate tire and tools needed
  2. Lossen the lugnuts
  3. Jack up car
  4. Take off all of the lugnuts
  5. Remove the lugnuts fully
  6. Take off the tire
  7. Put spare tire on
  8. Tighten the lugnuts, not all the way though
  9. Lower the car to the ground
  10. Tighten the lugnuts all of the way
    • Side mission step 1: Pat your self on the back becaue you are a boss
    • side mission step 2: Go to a tire store
    • side mission step 3: Either pay to have the tire fixed or buy a new tire and have them switch it out for the spare that you have.
  12. smile more
